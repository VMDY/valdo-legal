Privacy Policy for Valdo

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

1. Introduction

Valdo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our TikTok content scheduling application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Valdo, we collect the following information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive your TikTok username, profile information, and authentication tokens necessary to publish content on your behalf.
  • Content Data: Videos and associated metadata (captions, hashtags, scheduled times) that you upload to schedule for publishing.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with Valdo, including scheduling actions and publication logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and publish content on your behalf
  • Store and schedule your videos for publication at your chosen times
  • Maintain and improve Valdo's functionality
  • Provide customer support when needed

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your information only with:

  • TikTok: We use TikTok's official API to publish your content. Your data is transmitted directly to TikTok according to their privacy policy.
  • No Other Third Parties: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your TikTok authentication tokens are encrypted and stored securely.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke TikTok access permissions at any time
  • Export your data upon request

8. TikTok's Privacy Policy

Your use of TikTok through Valdo is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy. Please review it at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

9. Children's Privacy

Valdo is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
